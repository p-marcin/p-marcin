## ⚡ Software Engineer Senior Lead | Java | Enterprise Systems | Payments & FinTech

## 💬 About Me

Software Engineer Senior Lead with **10+ years of experience** developing enterprise payment systems in the financial technology sector.

Currently working at **Clear2Pay (an FIS company)**, where I contribute to the development of enterprise payment processing platforms used by financial institutions. My work focuses on implementing backend services in **Java** and supporting deployment of applications using **Docker, Kubernetes, and Helm**. I collaborate with cross-functional teams including Developers, QA Engineers, Technical Architects, Business Analysts, and Non-Functional Testing (NFT) teams, while coordinating with other platform teams whose systems integrate with or provide components for our solution.

My work contributes to real-time payment systems supporting high-value financial transactions within European banking infrastructure, focusing on the **SEPA Instant Credit Transfer (SCT Inst)** scheme.

Over the course of my career at FIS, I progressed from Junior Java Developer to Software Engineer Senior Lead, gaining deep experience in enterprise software development and payment processing platforms.

**Core expertise:**
- Java backend engineering
- Enterprise payment systems (SEPA Instant / SCT Inst)
- Backend services and enterprise system integration
- Financial technology infrastructure
- Containerized platforms (Docker, Kubernetes, Helm)

I enjoy working on complex backend systems and building reliable software for financial infrastructure.

**Always focused on the solution - not the problem.**
